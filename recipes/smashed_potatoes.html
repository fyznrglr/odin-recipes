Smashed Potatoes

Description

Extra crispy on the outside, fluffy on the inside! These buttery smashed potatoes will make you experience softness and crispiness with every bite. This recipe makes twelve, but I cannot guarantee that will be enough!

Let's start with the ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 700g / 1.4lb small potatoes (12 - 14)
    or
    1 - 1.2kg / 2 - 2.4lb medium potatoes (6 - 8)
  • 1 tbsp salt, for boiling
  • 30g / 2tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3/4 tsp salt, for sprinkling
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • finely chopped parsley, optional garnish
