Pasta Alla Trapanese (Sicilian Tomato Pesto)

Description

Fresh summer tomatoes meet classic Italian pasta in this amazing Sicilian pesto pasta recipe.

One of the best things about summer is that you can find sweet and bright-colored heirloom tomatoes that are immensely rich in flavor. They are great for eating as is, but how about trying them in this recipe to make a pasta that will send your taste buds through the roof? Let's start with the ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup whole roasted almonds
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup grated pecorino Romano cheese, put more to taste
  • 1 cup packed fresh basil leaves
  • 4 mint leaves (optional)
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound red cherry tomatoes or red grape tomatoes
  • 1 pound busiate pasta, or other curly shaped pasta, such as fusilli
